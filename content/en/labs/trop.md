The Realm of Patterns is a daily depth psychology practice powered by Jungian astrology and an 8-dimensional natal engine. Not horoscopes. A psychological mirror.

## The Core Loop

1. **You check in** — tell Sol (the AI narrator) how you're feeling in your own words
2. **Sol reads you** — connects what you just said to your natal psychology and today's planetary transits
3. **You see the pattern** — your 8-dimensional psychological profile visualized as a 3D SoulToroid

No other astrology product does this. Sol doesn't generate a generic reading — it reads YOUR words first, then explains them through your specific natal pattern.

## Privacy-First

- **No login required** — all personal data lives in localStorage
- **No tracking** — no analytics on personal readings
- **Shareable by token** — share your SoulToroid without sharing your data
- **Optional server sync** — only if you choose to
